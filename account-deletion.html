MieterSafe – Account Deletion Instructions

If you would like to delete your MieterSafe account and permanently remove all cloud-stored data associated with it, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

Send an email to our support team:

Email: support@mietersafe.de
Subject: “Delete My Account”

What Happens When You Request Deletion

Once we receive your request, we will:

  • Delete your MieterSafe user account from our authentication system.
  • Remove all cloud-stored data associated with your account, including:
    • Reports
    • Contacts
    • Damage notices
    • Waste schedules
    • Meter readings
    • Uploaded photos or attachments
  • Send you a confirmation email once deletion is complete.

Local-Only Users

If you use MieterSafe in local storage mode (no cloud sync enabled), your data is stored only on your device. In this case, deleting the app will remove your data.

Contact
